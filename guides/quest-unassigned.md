# Unassigned Quest Pages

### Quest, Current Quests, Finished Quests, Idiom Collection, Abortion

Press  Shortcut [Alt+Q] to open Quest Panel. There are 3 sub-panels:[Quest Complete],[Current Quest] and [Card Collection]

Quest Complete
To check quest completion evaluation. S level is the top level.

Current Quest
Click quest name to check quest description.

Card Collection
When you go up to Level 20, you can go to speak with Cloud City--Corey to accept card collection quest. Card Collection Quest has 5 quests per day.  One quest  for one card.  Cards are attached with special effect which can be used in combat.

Quest Track
Open Quest Panel, click the incomplete quest  to show quest descriptions on the right.

Abandon Quest
On [Current Quest], select the quest you want to abandon and then click [Abandon Quest] on the panel.
 When you abandon the quest, you may need to do it from the start. Think before you leap!!

 Quest Panel Abbreviation Tips [M]--[Main Quest];[B]--[Branch Quest];[Z]--[Zodiac Quest];[E]--[Event Quest];[H]--[Holiday Quest];[G]--[Guild Quest];[R]--[Recycle Quest];[L]--[Marriage Quest];[C]--[Card Quest];[D]--[Daily Quest].

### Brotherhood, Brotherhood Quests, Requirement for Brotherhood, Rules for Brotherhood

Brotherhood Requirement
The 2 players shall make a team(2 member only) and locate Brotherhood Manager to accept brotherhood task.

Brotherhood Rule
If one party of the 2 abandon the quest, quit the team or log off, the quest fails.

Brotherhood Quest
  Thick and Thin
Make a team and kill Enchanted Vampire.Then back to NPC to submit quest.
  Blood Oath
Kill Elite Goblin to bring Chain of Brotherhood back to NPC.
Your brotherhood is tested and you are brothers now!!
