# System Help

In-game help pages for game systems (`400xxx`–`401xxx`).

## Mini Map, Zodiac World Map, Map, Route
*Help ID prefix: 400017*

Map
Click [M] at top left corner of the interface or press  shortcut [Tab]to open map.

Map Function
 Every Round Mark at the map locates a NPC. They are marked with different colors according to their careers. By clicking [Quest] and [Function], the map will show Quest Npc and Function Npc. By clicking [NPC List], you can check NPC name at the map.

Locate NPC
Move the mouse to one round mark at the map to check NPC name and coordinates. Click the round mark to locate the NPC.

World Map
Click [W] at top left corner of the Map or press  shortcut [Alt+M]to open Zodiac World Map.

## Pet, Pet's Food, Pet's Attributes, Pet Talent, Pet Freedom, Pet Health
*Help ID prefix: 400004*

Shortcut to open Pet Panel: [ALT+W]

Summon Pet
Click [Summon] on Pet Panel,your pet will enter into the combat. If pet's level is higher than its owner, it will not join in the combat.

Pet Status Point
  + 4 Status points per level which can be added to Vitality, Intelligence, Strength and Dexterity.

Release Pet
Click [Release ] on Pet Panel to release your current pet.

Pet Health
Initial Health of Pet is 1000. Health will reduce when it combats, flees,or be killed. You can go to locate Cloud City--Pet Curer for Pet Health Potion.

Pet Talent
Click [Pet Talent] on Pet Panel to check pet talent status,pet item and pet skill.  F7-F8 are pet's fixed shortcuts.

Pet Show
Click [Set Pet Info] on pet panel and then press enter to show your pet in chat box.

## Team, Team Panel, New Team, Promotion, Dismission, Step Out, Team Invite, Return to Team, Team Infor.
*Help ID prefix: 400012*

Create Team
Click [Create Team] on team panel to create your team.

Invite To Team
There are at most 5 members in a team. Team leader can receive invitations from other players when there are not 5 members. Team leader can accept new members from [Applicants] on the left of Team Panel. Click  Accept  to accept players application and add them into teammates list. Click [Refuse] to refuse an applicatioin. Team Leader can invite other players by right-click the player. Click \"Invite To Team\".
When there are 5 members, team will not receive other players application.

Promote to Leader
  Team Leader can promote any member to be team leader.

Remove Member
Team leader can remove team members when not in combat scene.

Quit Team
Team leader will guide team member to walk. Team members can click [Quit] to quit team.
When quitting, a player can not be back.

Team Info
You can open this panel. Function will be available soon.

## Skill, Skill System, Skills Level, Skills Study, Skills Upgrading, Shortcuts of Skills
*Help ID prefix: 400003*

Press  Shortcut [ALT+S] to open skill panel.

Skill Type
There are Aptitude Skill, Normal Skill, Zodiac Skill, Passive Skill and Additional Skill.

Skill Train
Aptitude Skill: Aptitude Skill are skills of the aptitude you chose while creating your charactor. Click [Aptitude] to view all aptitude skills on the panel.Aptitude Skill can be upgraded to 12 level. LocateGrocerandFreddie to buy Gr.1 and Gr.2 skill books. You can accept Monster Encyclopedia quest to loote Gr.3 and Gr.4 Skill books at your 30 level.
    Normal Skill: All non-aptitude skills are normal skills. Normal skills can be learned from skill books. Normal skill can be upgraded to level 10.LocateGrocerandFreddie to buy Gr.1 and Gr.2 skill books. You can accept Monster Encyclopedia quest to loot Gr.3 and Gr.4 Skill books at your 30 level.
    Passive Skill:  Passive skill are closely related with Aptitude skill and normal skill. Passive skill will grow automatically with upgrading of aptitude and normal skill. Aptitude passive skill can be upgraded 1 level higher than normal passive skill.
    Zodiac Skill:At your 25 level, you can locate Cloud City--Commander to acceptZodiac MissionQuest. When you complete a quest,you will be rewarded a Zodiac skill. Zodiac skill consists of 2 types: attack skill(Metal, Water, Fire and Wood) and control skill.
    Additional Skill: Gathering skill, living skill, marriage skill and brothership skill are additional skills. These skills can not be learned from skill books. You can learn gathering skill and living skill from Housewife( refer to gathering system and living system for details.) Marriage skill and brotherhood skill will be mastered when the needed relationship are established.

Skill Grade
 Aptitude skill and normal skill are closely related with status. They shall obey the following roles.
    There are 6 grades for each skill and 3 skills for each grade. You can only learn 1 skill from the 3.[Gray Skill Icon] You can not learn it now.[Colorful Skill Icon]You have mastered it.

Skill Shortcut
Mastered skill be been shown in Mastered Skill Panel. Drag its icon into shortcut bar(F2-F6) on game interface to setup shortcuts.

## Relation, Add Friends, Chat, Friends Order, Mentor
*Help ID prefix: 400016*

Shortcut to open Relationship Panel: [ALT+F]

Add Friend
Click  Magnifiericon on Relationship Panel.Enter in player name and fill in message.Then click  Send . With the other's permit, he/she will be added in your friend list.

Friend Chat
Click Friendicon on Relation Panel to show Friend List. Click Friend() to show a drop down friend list. Double click the friend you want to chat with, a chat box will pop up.

Friend Option
Friend Option consists:Detailed Info, Remove, Drag to Blacklist, Remove from Blacklist and Mentor. Right click friend icon. Choose the option in drop down box.

Mentor
Right click friend icon and choose Mentor . When your request is accepted, your relationship became mentor and student. Meanwhile your information will be removed to Mentor and student list.

## Marriage, Requirements for Marriage, Rules for Marriage, Quest for Marriage
*Help ID prefix: 400005*

Marriage Requirement
The 2 payers shall make a team(2 members only) and locate Match-Maker  to accept marriage quest.

Marriage Rule
If one party of the 2 abandon the quest, quit the team or log off, the quest fails.

Marriage Quest
Common Enemy
Make a team and kill Jade Beast. Get Exquisite Stone back. Submit quest to NPC.
Common Challenge
Kill Ghost Fiend and back to NPC. NPC will give you a Keepsake.
Wedding Dress
 Bring the keepsake to Fairy lynn.Fairy Lynn needs Snow Sachet to weave the wedding dress with magic power of love.
Bride Veil
Joint efforts to kill Enchanted Beast and bring Snow Sachet to Fairy Lynn. Fairy Lynn will give you a Rainbow Garment in return.
Joint Hands
 Bring the Rainbow Garment to Match-Maker. Match-Maker will witness your true love and hold your wedding party!!

## Guild Quest
*Help ID prefix: 400006*

Guild Quest
    Daily Quest: Guild owner or vice guild owner can release daily quest per day. The quest will be randomly sent to online guild member. The acceptance member shall return to guild to complete guild quest at Presbyter Blair's. Members can also abandon the quest at quest panel. The quest will be randomly sent to another member.
Reward:

    Weekly Quest: Guild owner or vice guild owner can release weekly quest once per week. Weekly quest will be updated at Wendesday per week. It consists of several sub quests. Guild Owner or vice owner can release quest according to guild development.
Reward: Individual experience after quest and mana stone after weekly quest.

    Normal Quest: Accept normal quest at guild--Manager Emerson's. There are basic, senior and advanced normal quests.  Guild member can accept at most 5 times normal quest. Higher guild title can get accept more normal guild quest. The up-limit of normal quest is 21.
Reward: Individual experience and guild contribution after quest. You will get guild contribution , guild gold and individual experience after 3 quests. There is also a chance to get guild material and item reward. Reward item will be kept in guild warehouse. Guild member can exchange it by contribution. You can not see reward item status in warehouse. You can check it after getting it.
